Tulips

Tulips are known for their vibrant, cup-shaped flowers. They are native to the mountainous and steppeland of regions of Central Asia, extending to Eastern parts of Europe and Mediterranean.

Dandelion

Dandelion have a common name derived from the French for "lion's tooth". They are native to Eurasia and are now commonly found worldwide in lawns.

Highland Cattle

Cows that "live by the mountain" typically refers to breeds like the Highland Cattle from Scotland. They have adapted to rugged high-altitude environments.
